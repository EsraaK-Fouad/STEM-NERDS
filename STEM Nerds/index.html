Who are we?

STEM Nerds is a website that collects all materials the stemer will need from entering stem school until the end of grade 12.

Our goals

  • Help the stemers to find all what they need in one place.
  • Decrease the dispersion of the materials that found in many places and collect them.
  • Helping stemers to find what they want from materials in fastest and easiest way.
  • Make our website be the reference for any material the stemer will need.
  • Be the biggest collection of materials in STEM.
  • Be the best place to find all materials that related to stem in one place.
